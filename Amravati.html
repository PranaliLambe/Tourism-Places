shri ekvira devi temple,Amravati


Ekvira Devi Temple is situated in Amravati which is very near to the most famous temple in the district which is Ambadevi Temple. It is dedicated to Ekvira Devi (goddess) and was constructed during 1660’s by the son of Shri Janardan Swami. It is believed that Ekvira Devi is an incarnation of Shakti. One can either hire a taxi ,auto or a rickshaw to reach the temple or can climb up hill to attain darshan, to this place very enjoyable and adventurous .

Chikhaldara Hill station ,Amravati


Chikhaldara was discovered by Captain Robinson of the Hyderabad Regiment in 1823. The Englishmen found it particularly attractive because the lush green hue of the place reminded them of England. When the leaves fell in September/October, they were reminded of autumn in England. There was even a proposal to make it the seat of the Government of India.

Undavali caves, Amravati


This is the historical place in the district which is famous for its sandstone rock cut caves. These caves are built in ancient times and once there was a british rule. But now, it has become a famous tourist attraction in the city. This place is best for those people who really loves to get knowledge about our Indian history. So, if you are one of them, then, you must this place once.
